FLIGHT TRAINING

Welcome. Take your first step toward becoming a pilot.


At High Cloud Aviation, we focus on three things: safety, strong exam preparation, and training that is actually enjoyable. Whether you are starting from zero or working toward a career, our program is designed to build confident and capable pilots.


Train one-on-one with a Certified Flight Instructor (CFI) through structured, personalized lessons that combine flight and ground instruction. You will progress from basic flight skills to solo flight, cross-country navigation, and real-world flying in the Chicago airspace.


By the end of your training, you will be prepared to pass your FAA exams and operate safely and confidently at every stage of your aviation journey—from initial certification through advanced ratings and professional-level flying.

Why Train With Us

At High Cloud Aviation, training is built on transparency, safety, and efficiency. Our aircraft are maintained to a high standard, with maintenance records that are clearly documented and available for review.


You will train one-on-one with a dedicated instructor, gaining real-world experience in the Chicago airspace while developing strong decision-making and flying skills.


We offer competitive, affordable pricing with no hidden fees, along with a flexible scheduling system that allows you to train consistently around your availability. Our program emphasizes safety, exam readiness, and steady progression—providing a clear and practical path from your first flight through advanced ratings.
